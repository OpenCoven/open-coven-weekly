<div class="label">Open Source Moment</div>

# GLM-5.2: the *vibe check* it passed.

- By **Zhipu AI / Z.ai** — shipped June 17–18, 2026
- 753B total params MoE, ~40B active per token, **MIT license**
- Trained on **28.5 trillion tokens**, 1M context window
- **New arch trick: IndexShare** — reuses sparse-attention top-k indices across layer groups → cheap 1M-token inference
- Jeremy Howard (fast.ai): "at least as good as Opus 4.8 and GPT-5.5 for my work"
- Artificial Analysis AA-Briefcase benchmark: **GLM-5.2 at $2.40/task** vs Fable 5 at $31/task
- r/LocalLLaMA exploding — first open model to pass the "daily driver" vibe check
- **Catch:** needs ~890GB VRAM at FP8, ~180GB with 4-bit quant. Your Mac Mini won't run it. Your GB10 cluster might.
- Z.ai forecast: open Fable-class model by December 2026 🤯
