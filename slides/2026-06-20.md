# Nemotron 3 Ultra: *the open heavyweight.*

<div class="cols-2">
<div>

**The model:**
- **NVIDIA** — 550B total · 55B active params
- Hybrid **Transformer-Mamba-MoE** architecture
- Up to **1M context** · NVFP4 quant on build.nvidia.com
- Fully permissive open license
- Live on **OpenRouter** · local docs from **Unsloth**

</div>
<div>

**Why the hybrid arch matters:**
- Mamba = state-space model → better long-context scaling than pure attention
- MoE = sparse activation → more params, same compute
- Combining all three is a deliberate research bet

**The pitch:**
- Agent-native, designed for multi-agent orchestration
- Every major lab now has an open flagship play

> *"At this rate the MoE will need its own MoE"* — Reddit 💀

</div>
</div>

<!--
