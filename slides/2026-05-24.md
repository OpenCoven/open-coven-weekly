<div class="label">API deep dive</div>

# The *Coven* socket API.

<div style="display:grid;grid-template-columns:1fr 1fr;gap:14px;max-width:880px;margin-top:1.2rem">
  <div class="card">
    <div class="card-title">Handshake</div>
    <p><code>GET /api/v1/health</code> confirms <code>apiVersion: "coven.daemon.v1"</code> before a client assumes response shapes.</p>
  </div>
  <div class="card">
    <div class="card-title">Discovery</div>
    <p><code>GET /api/v1/capabilities</code> tells clients which adapters, policies, and action ids are available.</p>
  </div>
  <div class="card">
    <div class="card-title">Sessions</div>
    <p><code>POST /api/v1/sessions</code> launches a daemon-validated harness session inside a project root.</p>
  </div>
  <div class="card">
    <div class="card-title">Events</div>
    <p><code>GET /api/v1/events</code> returns append-only session history with <code>afterSeq</code> cursor replay.</p>
  </div>
</div>

<div class="promise">
  <p>No OAuth, no bearer token, no browser cookie. The local socket and private Coven home are the boundary.</p>
</div>
