<!--
AGENTS — This is the section I want to spend the most time on, because the pattern here is significant.

Microsoft Scout is "always-on" — not a tool you invoke, but an agent running continuously in the background across your Microsoft 365 apps. That's a fundamentally different relationship with AI than "open ChatGPT, ask a question."

Project Solara takes it further — a platform for AI-first devices where agents are the primary interface. Not apps with AI features. Agents instead of apps. That framing is worth sitting with.

The Pinecone × OneLake integration is less flashy but arguably more important for enterprise: AI agents querying structured knowledge stores directly, without chunking everything through an LLM. Faster, cheaper, more accurate.

And Claude Code is quietly the product story of the year — driving the majority of Anthropic's $30B run rate. Agentic coding isn't a feature anymore, it's the core value proposition.

The through-line: agents aren't experimental anymore. They're becoming the infrastructure layer.

But the real story is the engine: Apple is powering the new Siri with Google Gemini under a multi-year deal. Not their own model. Not OpenAI. Google.
